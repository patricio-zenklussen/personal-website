Curiosity is not a trait you either have or don't. It's a practice. Something you cultivate by paying attention to the things that make you pause.

## The compound effect

Every question leads to another. Every book opens a door to three more. This is not overwhelming — it's the point.

## Staying open

The moment you decide you know enough about something is the moment you stop growing. The best conversations I've had were with people who approached familiar topics as if they were encountering them for the first time.

> "The important thing is not to stop questioning." — Albert Einstein
